Homebrew sanity rule: WIS save DC 12 at the start of each turn while active. On a fail, the DM narrates a moment of blankness — Kairon's eyes go grey, he moves wrong, he reaches for someone who isn't there. Halo of Spores
Reaction · Passive▶
When a creature you can see moves within 10ft of you (or starts its turn there), use your Reaction: that creature makes a CON save DC 13 or takes 1d4 necrotic (1d6 during Symbiotic Entity). No cost — just your Reaction. Use it every time an enemy enters your space. It's free damage.
Wild Shape
2 uses · Short Rest▶
Transform into a beast you've seen (CR ½ or lower, no fly/swim speed at level 3). Gain that beast's HP, AC, and physical abilities. Your mental stats stay the same. You can still concentrate on spells. Ends if reduced to 0 HP (you revert with your HP). Kairon rarely uses this — he uses his uses for Symbiotic Entity instead. But a scout form (spider, weasel, etc.) can be useful.
Breath Weapon — Necrotic Line
Grey Dragonborn · Action▶
Action: exhale a 30ft line (5ft wide). All creatures in the line make a CON save DC 12. Fail: 2d10 necrotic. Pass: half. 3 uses per Long Rest. On a hit, the grey-black vapour looks disturbingly like Calamity Fog — which other party members may notice.
Necrotic Resistance
Grey Dragonborn · Passive▶
You have resistance to necrotic damage — you take half damage from all necrotic sources including the Blight's effects. This is why Kairon can study the undead so closely without being destroyed by them.
Druidic Language
▶
You know Druidic — a secret language only druids know. You can leave messages in nature (carvings, twisted branches, pebble arrangements) that only other druids can read. Also useful for secretly communicating with Liora, who may know some nature lore.
